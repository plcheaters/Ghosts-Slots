Promotions & Selling

Advertising Restrictions
No unsolicited advertising on other Discord servers, Telegram channels, social media, or any external site via our server channels.
Do not post invite links, referral codes, or promotional banners in our server channels.
Spamming mass‑mentions or @everyone/@here for promotions is strictly prohibited.
Verified Sellers Only
Only users with a verified “slot holder” status may offer services or list products for sale.
To become verified, complete our onboarding and identity‑verification process.
No DM‑Based Solicitation
Directly messaging members to pitch services, divert tickets, or funnel users to external platforms is forbidden.
All offers must be made in the designated slot channels only.
External Redirects Banned
You may not redirect users to your own Discord server, Telegram, website, or any other third‑party platform for sales or support.
Violations will lead to immediate suspension of selling privileges and potential server ban.
Giveaway & Rewards
Giveaway Rewards: All giveaway rewards for events must be claimed within one hour of the event's conclusion, unless the host decides otherwise.
Non‑Transferable Rewards: Rewards are strictly non‑transferable and cannot be exchanged, redeemed for cash, or assigned to any other individual.
Requirements: Rewards are only given if the requirements are fulfilled (may be specified during or after the giveaway).
Eligibility Verification: Participants must provide valid proof of eligibility; failure may result in disqualification.
Affiliate & Referral Links
Affiliate or referral links are only allowed if pre‑approved by staff and confined to the affiliate‑program channel.
Any undisclosed or deceptive use of referral links will result in penalties.
Transparency & Disclosure
All listings must clearly state pricing, deliverables, and any additional fees up front.
Your terms of service should not contradict your advertisement (e.g., selling a 30‑day product with a 7‑day warranty is not allowed).
Prohibited Promotions
No pyramid schemes, MLM solicitations, or get‑rich‑quick scams.
Illegal content—pirated software, unauthorized copyrighted material, or illicit services—is strictly forbidden.
Compliance & Conduct
Adhere to Discord’s Terms of Service and all applicable laws in your promotions.
Harassment, threats, or intimidation used to coerce sales or persuade users is zero‑tolerance.
Consequences
First offense: formal warning and temporary mute from promotion channels.
Repeat violations: loss of verification, removal of selling privileges, and permanent ban.

Possible Sanctions: Slot revocation, monetary fines, downgrades, temporary suspensions, blacklisting from Ghost Slots and its affiliates.
Staff Authority: Staff may impose any penalty—without notice or explanation—on users exhibiting suspicious, abusive, or rule‑breaking behavior.

Overstocking & Misadvertising: Overproducing, overselling, misadvertising, or listing more keys than you have in stock is strictly prohibited. Any such activity may result in immediate slot revocation without warning.

Report Freeze: Any open report will suspend your slot’s visibility and activity.
12‑Hour Response: You have 12 hours to address a report; failure to respond triggers automatic slot revocation.
Multiple Reports Risk: Accumulating three valid reports—even if overturned later—puts your slot at risk of removal.

Punishment & Violation

Verbal Warning
Issued for first‑time or minor infractions (e.g., off‑topic posts, minor etiquette slips).
Temporary Restrictions
Mute from chat channels or loss of posting privileges for 24–72 hours for repeated minor offenses or medium‑severity breaches (e.g., unsolicited DMs, light spam).
Suspension
Temporary ban from the server (3–7 days) for serious rule violations, including:
• Continued harassment or toxic behavior after warning
• Attempting to manipulate staff or game the dispute process
• Unauthorized copying or cloning of server assets
Permanent Ban
Immediate, irrevocable removal for the most severe offenses, such as:
• Doxxing, threats of hacking, or leaking private information
• Fraudulent chargebacks, payment disputes, or financial scams
• Impersonation of staff, admins, or bots
• Public exposure of internal discussions or audit logs
Blacklist from Marketplace
Loss of all selling privileges, escrow access, and promotional rights.
Blacklisted users cannot create new listings or participate in transactions.
Escalation & Legal Referral
Cases involving illegal activities (e.g., distributing malware, facilitating illicit trade, severe harassment) will be documented and referred to law enforcement or payment networks as required.
Public Disclosure
In instances of major fraud or repeated violations, the offender’s username and offense summary may be posted in the server’s #offenses log for community awareness.
Appeals Process
If you believe a punishment was issued in error, submit an appeal via a support ticket within 24 hours.
Appeals will be reviewed by senior staff; decisions are final.
